What can I do in Utrera?

Consolation Fair

Considered the second most important fair in Andalusia after Seville's, it is held the first week of September. If you feel like experiencing the Andalusian fair with flamenco, guitars, singing, and many more things, you can't miss it.

Town Hall

Although it may seem like a strange proposal, Utrera's town hall was the palace of the Counts of Vistahermosa, hence it looks nothing like the concept you have of a normal town hall. In addition to a covered patio with nineteenth-century columns and stained glass windows, the town hall has rooms in Arabic, Chinese, and even Roman style.

Friendlyfire Paintball Camp

If you want an experience that is not so much about visiting but more about participating, here you have a paintball field with a spectacular setting and a great team of professionals who will help you with whatever you need.

Gómez Mier Tavern

After the action, you'll get hungry and you'll have to go eat somewhere, right? Well, here you have the Gómez Mier tavern, where they will serve you some cold cuts to nibble on with a good atmosphere and a nice cold beer.
