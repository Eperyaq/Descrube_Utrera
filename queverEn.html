What to See in Utrera?

Sanctuary of Our Lady of Consolation

Sanctuary originating from a mid-16th century hermitage located in a very close place to Utrera, known for its lively history that includes hermitages, convent of friars minor, and image of the Virgin of Consolation with a reputation for miracles, the truly interesting thing about this is the interior.

Church of Santiago

Beautiful church located in the center of Utrera, both for the simplicity of its lines, high archivolts, impressive high altar, beautiful side chapels, it is located in a prominent area, where you can go up to visit the chapel from where you can see beautiful views of Utrera. You can pay €2 for a guided tour where they will tell you the history of the church.

Santa Maria de la Mesa

Located in the heart of the neighborhood of Sta Maria, it stands out for its main altarpiece by Francisco Balleteros. Among its treasures is a small painting, the Virgin with the Child, San Roque and San Sebastián by Bernardino.

Old Jewish Quarter

A very beautiful street to walk along with typical decoration featuring pots with beautiful flowers. Located in the Santa Cruz neighborhood, it showcases history in its narrow streets and is famous for its interior courtyards.
