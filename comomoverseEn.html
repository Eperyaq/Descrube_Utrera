How can I get around Utrera?

Public Transport

Encouraging non-pollution, our first recommendation is to use public transportation to get around Utrera as you won't pollute and you'll move quickly to the places you want to go. You can find the bus schedule here.

Walking

Considering that Utrera is not that big, you could access several places by walking. You might not be able to go from one end of Utrera to the other, but you could go to places nearby where you're staying.

By Taxi

Easy and quick option if you need to get somewhere fast or don't feel like taking public transport although you'll pollute!
