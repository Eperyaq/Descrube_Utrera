Besana Tapas

Besana Tapas is a place of innovative cuisine located in an alley of Utrera. Launched in 2009, Besana Tapas has become one of the most well-known restaurants in all of Utrera, with very good reviews, recommended by both tourists and locals.


Recommendations:
Tuna and truffle bun
Sirloin tataki
Besana salad
Squid croquettes in ink
Roast suckling pig


Ajolote Bar

From Mexico to your table, I present to you Ajolote Bar, a very good option to attend if you crave Mexican food. This bar is very famous for its delicious Mexican cuisine and its good reputation and reviews. It has excellent service where you are treated like at home, highly recommended, don't miss it!


Recommendations:
Ajolotito
Acapulco Wrap
Yucatan Burrito
Cochinita pibil burger
Pizzas

Casa Diego Tapas

At Casa Diego, we find a wide variety of Spanish, rather Mediterranean food with a beautiful presentation and fabulous flavor. At Casa Diego, they have a very professional service where they can recommend types of dishes if you can't decide on any.


Recommendations:
Tuna loin
Cheek canelone
Beet salmorejo
Tuna toast
Tuna tataki

Siddartha

At Siddartha, we find a wide variety of Spanish cuisine, with very good reviews praising the deliciousness of everything and the excellent service provided by both the chefs and the waiters.


Recommendations:
Snails
Goat cheese
Chicken wings
Potato omelette
Homemade croquettes
